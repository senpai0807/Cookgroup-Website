About Us

We're a group of resellers wanting to give back to the reselling community by doing what we can to help you on your journey as a reseller be as easy as possible.
Below are features we currently have available within our server!

ACO

ACOs or "Autocheckouts" means that if you are unable to run the drop yourself, we can run for you! All you do is send us your billing information and you can go on with your day and we'll hit for you!

Calendars

We understand that not everyone is readily available to just run for any drop. With the help of our daily and weekly updated calendars, you can stay up to date and know when a release is happening so you can fit it in your schedule!

1 on 1 Support

Botting or reselling in general can be very difficult to learn and sometimes you might not even know where to start. With the help of our experienced staff members, we can answer all the questions you may have so you can have a better understanding on how it all works!

Server

Our Discord Server is a community based server. Within Boombap's Cookout, you're not stuck with just talking about all things botting and reselling.
You can join to talk about absolutely anything!
